Let's Talk about Zamboli

Who is Zamboli?

Zamboli is a house cat that lives with me. He is orange and very smart. He may as well be the vice president one day, similar to the popular internet cat named Oscar. I will miss him immensely.

Why is Zamboli special?

Zamboli has gone through a lot. At one point, he managed to beat Feline Infectious Peritonitis (FIP). It's known as an "untreatable" virus that we managed to treat for a long period of time. Well done Zam!
