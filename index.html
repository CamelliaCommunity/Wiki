Let's Talk about Zamboli

Who is Zamboli?

Zamboli is a house cat that lives with me. He is orange and very smart. He may as well be the vice president one day, similar to the popular internet cat named Oscar.

Why is Zamboli special?

Zamboli has gone through a lot, he managed to beat Feline Infectious Peritonitis (FIP). It's known as an "untreatable" virus that we managed to treat. Well done Zam!
